PUGS

The Pug is a breed of dog, similar to a bulldog, with a wrinkled face, short snub and curled tail. It has a short, fine glossy coat that ranges in colors, from brown, black, golden and a mixture of them all. Their snouts are usually black and have flapped ears.

Pugs were brought from China to Europe in the sixteenth century and were popyularized in WEster Europe by the House of Orange of the Netherlands..

They are known for being social and gentle and tend to make great companion dogs. They are usually friendly and great with kids. Although they are particularly muscular, they are fun to play with and people of all ages can play with them.

It is still popular in the 21st century and even has its own community where memes and pages are generated like here

  • Average Height: 10-11"
  • Function: Lap Dog/Companion
